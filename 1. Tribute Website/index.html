Imran Khan

1952 - Forever

The Greatest Pakistani

Imran Khan, born on November 25, 1952, is a prominent Pakistani politician and former cricketer who served as the 22nd Prime Minister of Pakistan from August 2018 to April 2022. Renowned for leading Pakistan to its first and only Cricket World Cup victory in 1992, Khan transitioned from sports to politics, founding the Pakistan Tehreek-e-Insaf (PTI) party in 1996. His political career has been marked by a focus on anti-corruption, economic reforms, and social justice. Despite facing numerous challenges, including economic instability and political opposition, Khan's tenure saw efforts to improve Pakistan's socio-economic conditions. His charismatic leadership and determination continue to influence Pakistan's political landscape.
